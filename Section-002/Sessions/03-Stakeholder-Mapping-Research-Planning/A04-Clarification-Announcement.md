Clarification on A04 (Causal Loop Map)

Hey everyone, a few of you had questions last night about the causal loop map, so I wanted to clear something up.

Your causal loop map should be about your team's chosen problem space, the same scenario you selected in Session 2 and are researching for A03.

The drafts you sketched last night were meant to be exactly that: drafts. Think of them as a stake in the ground. The whole point was to get your thinking started before the research period so you're not going into interviews and observations cold. Nobody's causal loop should look polished right now.

Over the next two weeks, what you learn from stakeholders will challenge and reshape what you drafted. You'll discover nodes you didn't think of, loops that work differently than you assumed, and dynamics that surprise you. That's the process working.

Start from your rough draft, stay curious, and let the research do its job.
