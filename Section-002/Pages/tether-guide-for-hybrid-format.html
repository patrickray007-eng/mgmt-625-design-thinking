TETHER Guide for Hybrid Format

Your Environment is the Asset

In this hybrid course, you'll observe in your own context rather than a shared campus location. This means our class will have observations from:

  • Different workplaces and industries
  • Different cities and neighborhoods
  • Different types of public spaces
  • Different times of day and week

Choosing Your Location

Pick somewhere you can:

  • Observe for 90 uninterrupted minutes
  • See people interacting with spaces, systems, or each other
  • Sit or stand comfortably without being disruptive

Good options:

  • Your workplace common area, lobby, or break room
  • A local coffee shop during busy hours
  • Public transit (station or during a commute)
  • Grocery store or retail environment
  • Community space, library, or park

What Makes Your Context Valuable

  • You have insider access others don't
  • You understand background context
  • You can notice things an outsider would miss
  • Your observations complement others' different contexts
