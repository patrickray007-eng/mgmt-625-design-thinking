TETHER = Take Extended Time to Hold Everything in Review

The Hybrid Advantage: Unlike a traditional course, you'll observe in YOUR environment—your workplace, a local coffee shop, public transit, your neighborhood. The diversity of contexts across our class is a feature, not a limitation.

Key details:

  • 90 minutes of uninterrupted observation
  • Choose a location where you can observe people interacting with spaces, systems, or each other
  • No phone, no laptop—just you and your notebook
  • Document what you SEE, not what you THINK it means
  • Include a half-page reflection afterward
