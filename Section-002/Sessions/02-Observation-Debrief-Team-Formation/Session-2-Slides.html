Scenario vs. Subject

A scenario is the broad problem space. A subject is your team's specific investigation within it.

Examples

  • Scenario A + Subject: "How do new nurses navigate their first rotation at a community hospital?"
  • Scenario D + Subject: "Why do eligible families in one school district not use the free tutoring program?"

How to Choose Well

  • Pick a subject where your team can access real stakeholders
  • Keep it bounded: one organization, one location, or one population
  • Follow genuine curiosity
  • Aim for no more than 3 stakeholder groups
